Powerful Integrated HCM Solutions for the Mid-Market

Ascentis automates and streamlines core business processes that save time and money.

HRIS and Benefits Management

Ascentis HR simplifies and automates the management of employee data and benefits, reducing costs and streamlining complex HR administrative processes.

Payroll and Tax Filing Services

Ascentis Payroll allows you to process payroll in real-time, ensuring accuracy, flexibility and complete control. Processing time is reduced by as much as 30%.

Talent Management

Ascentis Talent Management offers a suite of powerful tools including performance and learning management, and compensation planning, all integrated with Ascentis HR.

Applicant Tracking and Onboarding

Ascentis Recruiting sources the right candidates, increases the quality of your applicants and pre-screens applicants online so you get the right candidate at the right time.

Time and Attendance

Ascentis Timekeeper software specializes in managing labor costs, employee scheduling, resource utilization, risk mitigation, error reduction, and more.

ACA Compliance

Ascentis is dedicated to delivering the tools you need to seamlessly and easily integrate the mandates of the ACA into your business processes.
